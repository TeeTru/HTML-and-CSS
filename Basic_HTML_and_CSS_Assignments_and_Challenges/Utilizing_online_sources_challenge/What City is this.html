How Great is Salt Lake City?

PROS

This is one of the best cities to live in America if you are planning on raising a family. It's also one of the best cities if you are single and actively enjoy the outdoors. You also get to experience all 4 seasons.

CONS

Now some people would say it get's too cold in the winter, thats according to the residents who dont skii or snowboard. The air quality here can be extremely bad because of the "bowl" affect where polution can not escape the valley. The cost of living is also expensive compared to what it was some years back.
